Overview

To nurture and sustain an excellent research ambience conducive to the development and growth of committed
professionals for sustainable development of the nation and to accomplish its integration into the global economy
is always given high importance at Anurag University. To facilitate and contribute to research in all disciplines, the
Universityis focusing more on Research Programs with Commitment to excellence in fundamental research as well
as the development of innovative technologies for the future. A reputation for excellence in research supported by a
high caliber staff is reflected in the demand for entry to Anurag university programs from high achieving students.
Anurag University offers a quality research experience for its students
